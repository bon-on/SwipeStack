# Swipe Stack Privacy Policy Draft

Swipe Stack is an offline single-player arcade game. The game stores your best
stack count locally on your device.

## Data handled by the app

- Best stack count: stored locally through platform preferences.
- Advertising data: Google AdMob may process device identifiers, approximate
  device information, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and related data to load
  ads, limit abuse, measure performance, and support ad delivery.

## Third-party services

- Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads SDK
